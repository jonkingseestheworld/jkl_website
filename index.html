Cognitive Neuroscientist // Programming Enthusiast // Data Analyst

Hi there, thanks for visiting my page. I am Johnny Lau and I am passionate about using an experimental testing and data-driven approach to address real-life questions. I have a keen interest in data science & use of tech in healthcare.

I am currently a postdoc research fellow at the Motivation Science Lab of the University of Reading, UK. My research is primarily concerned with understanding how decisions are made and motivated.

My work uses a combination of experimental investigation, statistical model testing and neuroimaging to examine the neurobiological mechanism underlying how intrinsic motivation (like curiosity) biases us in making risky decisions. I also have experience in applying a multivariate pattern recognition approach (incorporated with machine learning algorithm) on the analysis of brain imaging data to predict choice behaviours.
